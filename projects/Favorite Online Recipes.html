Bánh Cuốn

Bánh Cuốn is a veitnamese noodle dish similar to the Cantonese cheung fun. It is a large flat noodle stuffed typically with pork and a special dried mushroom. It is relatively easy to make and very tasty. served with lots of fresh herbs and bean sprouts, this dish is very versatile and will impress anyone who comes over for dinner.
